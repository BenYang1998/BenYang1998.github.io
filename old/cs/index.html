Rutgers

Below is a journey through my dedication and bond to dear old Rutgers, my home for the last three years. Here's to another lifetime of friendship and community with the Scarlet Knights...

History

My first encounter with Rutgers was through the Marching Scarlet Knights, founded in 1915 and a part of the university for more than 100 years. I learned the historical anthems of the university and time-old traditions of our athletics and band. [Top Row, 3rd from left.]

Spirit

A great deal of my Rutgers spirit & personality came from the people I met at HackRU. Together, we managed and raised a 800-person event in the name of Rutgers tech and hacking. I wouldn't be the same person without my amazing friends. [Top Row, in the middle with the hat.]

Tradition

While there are a number of traditions at Rutgers, the one that matters most to me is teaching a FIGS. Even though the program was only founded in 2000, my experience with this tradition and teaching 25 bright freshmen is one of the highlights of my life. [Top Row, 6th from the left.]
